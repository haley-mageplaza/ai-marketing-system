# Funnel Strategy – Mageplaza
## Purpose
This document defines the marketing funnel structure for Mageplaza.

It is used to:
- Classify content by funnel stage (TOFU, MOFU, BOFU)
- Guide SEO and content strategy
- Optimize internal linking
- Align content with conversion goals

All marketing tasks MUST reference this funnel.

---

# 1. Overview

Mageplaza operates in two main areas:
- Magento Extensions (products)
- Magento Services (solutions)

The funnel must guide users from:
👉 Awareness → Consideration → Decision → Conversion

---

# 2. Funnel Stages

## 2.1 TOFU – Awareness Stage

### Goal
Attract new users who:
- Have a problem but don’t know the solution yet
- Are researching broadly
- Are not ready to buy

---

### Content Types

- Guides (How-to, tutorials)
- Definitions (What is…)
- Trend articles
- Problem-based content
- Educational blog posts

---

### Example Topics

- What is Magento 2?
- How to improve eCommerce performance
- Why checkout optimization matters
- Magento vs Shopify comparison
- Common eCommerce challenges

---

### SEO Intent
- Informational
- Broad keywords
- High volume, lower conversion

---

### Content Rules

- Focus on clarity and education
- Avoid hard selling
- Introduce problems clearly
- Build trust and authority

---

### Internal Linking Rules

TOFU content MUST:
- Link to 1–2 MOFU articles
- Link to at least 1 BOFU page (soft CTA)

---

### CTA Style

Soft CTA:
- Learn more
- Explore solutions
- See how it works

---

# 2.2 MOFU – Consideration Stage

### Goal
Help users:
- Evaluate solutions
- Compare options
- Understand how to solve their problem

---

### Content Types

- Comparison articles
- Best tools / extensions
- Use case content
- Feature breakdowns
- Strategy guides

---

### Example Topics

- Best Magento 2 extensions for checkout
- Magento 2 One Step Checkout comparison
- How to choose a Magento extension
- Magento performance optimization strategies

---

### SEO Intent
- Commercial investigation
- Medium competition
- Higher conversion potential

---

### Content Rules

- Be specific and practical
- Highlight differences between solutions
- Include pros & cons
- Show real use cases

---

### Internal Linking Rules

MOFU content MUST:
- Link to TOFU (supporting content)
- Link strongly to BOFU pages (main goal)

---

### CTA Style

Moderate CTA:
- Try extension
- View features
- Request demo
- See pricing

---

# 2.3 BOFU – Decision Stage

### Goal
Convert users into:
- Customers (extensions)
- Leads (services)

---

### Content Types

- Product pages (extensions)
- Service pages
- Case studies
- Pricing pages
- Demo pages

---

### Example Pages

- Magento 2 Reward Points extension
- Magento 2 Store Locator extension
- Magento Development Services
- Magento Migration Services

---

### SEO Intent
- Transactional
- High conversion intent
- Lower volume but high value

---

### Content Rules

- Focus on benefits and outcomes
- Clear value proposition
- Strong differentiation
- Address objections

---

### Must Include

- Features & benefits
- Screenshots / visuals
- Use cases
- FAQs
- Clear CTA

---

### Internal Linking Rules

BOFU pages MUST:
- Link to supporting MOFU content
- Link to related products/services
- Reinforce topical authority

---

### CTA Style

Strong CTA:
- Buy now
- Get started
- Book consultation
- Talk to experts

---

# 4. Internal Linking Strategy (Critical)

## 4.1 Funnel Flow

Correct flow:

TOFU → MOFU → BOFU

---

## 4.2 Linking Rules

### TOFU
- Link to MOFU (primary)
- Link to BOFU (secondary)

### MOFU
- Link to BOFU (primary)
- Link to TOFU (supporting)

### BOFU
- Link to MOFU (support)
- Link to related BOFU pages

---

## 4.3 Anchor Text Rules

- Use descriptive anchors
- Include keywords naturally
- Avoid generic phrases

---

# 5. Content Evaluation by Funnel

When auditing content, always determine:

- Funnel stage (TOFU / MOFU / BOFU)
- Whether it matches search intent
- Whether it links correctly within funnel

---

## 5.1 Common Issues

❌ TOFU content with no internal links  
❌ MOFU content not linking to product/service  
❌ BOFU content lacking supporting content  
❌ Mixed intent pages  

---

# 6. Funnel-Based Content Strategy

## 6.1 TOFU Strategy
- Build traffic
- Target broad keywords
- Educate users

---

## 6.2 MOFU Strategy
- Capture interest
- Build authority
- Position Mageplaza as solution

---

## 6.3 BOFU Strategy
- Convert users
- Highlight value
- Reduce friction

---

# 7. Final Rule

Every piece of content MUST:

- Belong to a funnel stage
- Serve a clear purpose
- Connect to the next stage

If a page does not move users forward → it is ineffective.
