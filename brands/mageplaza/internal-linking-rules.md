# Internal Linking Rules & Strategy
## Purpose
This document defines how internal links must be structured across all content and pages.

Goals:
- Strengthen SEO (topical authority & crawlability)
- Improve user navigation and engagement
- Support conversion funnels (TOFU → MOFU → BOFU)
- Help AI systems (AIO, GEO) understand content relationships

All content MUST follow these rules.

---

# 1. Core Principles

## 1.1 Every Page Must Link Out
No page should be isolated.

Each page MUST:
- Link to at least 2–5 relevant internal pages
- Include at least:
  - 1 contextual (same topic) link
  - 1 funnel (conversion-oriented) link

---

## 1.2 Links Must Be Contextual
Links must:
- Fit naturally within the sentence
- Extend the current idea
- Help users explore deeper

❌ Avoid forced or random links  
❌ Avoid dumping links at the end without context  

---

## 1.3 Relevance Over Quantity
Do NOT add links just to increase count.

Each link must:
- Be relevant to the current section
- Provide additional value

Rule:
> If the link does not help the reader → remove it

---

## 1.4 Anchor Text Must Be Descriptive
Use meaningful anchor text.

❌ Bad:
- "click here"
- "learn more"
- "this article"

✅ Good:
- "Magento 2 store pickup solution"
- "Shopify B2B pricing guide"

---

# 2. Funnel-Based Linking (Critical)

Internal linking MUST support funnel movement.

---

## 2.1 TOFU (Top of Funnel)

Content type:
- Guides
- Definitions
- Educational blogs

Must link to:
- Related TOFU articles (cluster)
- At least 1 MOFU or BOFU page

Purpose:
→ Move users deeper into the funnel

---

## 2.2 MOFU (Middle of Funnel)

Content type:
- Comparisons
- Best tools
- Use cases
- Feature breakdowns

Must link to:
- TOFU articles (supporting knowledge)
- BOFU pages (product/service)

Purpose:
→ Help users evaluate options

---

## 2.3 BOFU (Bottom of Funnel)

Content type:
- Product pages
- Service pages
- Case studies

Must link to:
- Supporting blog content (TOFU/MOFU)
- Related products/services

Purpose:
→ Increase trust and conversion

---

# 3. Linking Patterns

## 3.1 Hub & Cluster Model

Each topic should follow:

- 1 pillar page (main topic)
- Multiple cluster articles (supporting topics)

Rules:
- Clusters link to pillar
- Pillar links to clusters
- Clusters interlink where relevant

---

## 3.2 Cross-Linking Between Related Topics

Link across topics when:
- They share audience
- They support the same goal

Example:
- SEO article → link to conversion optimization guide
- Shopify B2B → link to pricing guide

---

## 3.3 Avoid Orphan Pages

Every page must:
- Receive at least 1 internal link from another page

---

# 4. Placement Rules

## 4.1 Where to Place Links

Best locations:
- Within paragraphs (preferred)
- After explaining a concept
- Inside examples or use cases

Optional:
- In bullet lists
- In CTA sections

---

## 4.2 Where NOT to Place Links

Avoid:
- Random link blocks
- Overloaded paragraphs
- Footer-only links

---

## 4.3 Frequency Guidelines

General rule:
- 1 link per 150–300 words

Avoid:
- Over-linking in short sections
- Too many links in one paragraph

---

# 5. Anchor Text Strategy

## 5.1 Types of Anchor Text

Use a mix of:

- Exact match  
  "Magento 2 reward points"

- Partial match  
  "reward points system for Magento"

- Natural phrase  
  "how to set up reward points"

---

## 5.2 Avoid Over-Optimization

Do NOT:
- Repeat the same anchor text excessively
- Force keywords unnaturally

---

## 5.3 Keep It Natural

Anchor text must:
- Fit the sentence
- Sound like normal writing

---

# 6. Strategic Linking by Page Type

## 6.1 Blog Articles

Must include:
- 2–3 links to related blogs
- 1–2 links to product/service pages

---

## 6.2 Product Pages

Must include:
- Links to:
  - Related features
  - Documentation
  - Use case blogs

---

## 6.3 Service Pages

Must include:
- Links to:
  - Case studies
  - Related services
  - Educational blogs

---

## 6.4 App / Listing Pages (Shopify ASO)

Must include:
- Links to:
  - Feature explanations
  - Use cases
  - Blog content

---

# 7. SEO & AI Optimization

## 7.1 Help Search Engines Understand Structure

Internal links should:
- Indicate content hierarchy
- Show topic relationships
- Reinforce keyword relevance

---

## 7.2 Support AI Extraction (AIO, GEO)

Links should:
- Connect related concepts clearly
- Help AI map entities and relationships

---

## 7.3 Improve Crawlability

Ensure:
- Important pages are linked multiple times
- Deep pages are reachable within 3 clicks

---

# 8. Conversion-Focused Linking

## 8.1 Always Include a Next Step

Every page should guide users to:
- Another article
- A product/service page
- A CTA

---

## 8.2 Link With Intent

Each link should answer:
> Why should the user click this?

---

## 8.3 Reduce Drop-Off

Use links to:
- Clarify confusion
- Provide deeper context
- Lead to solutions

---

# 9. Common Mistakes to Avoid

❌ Linking just for SEO  
❌ Using generic anchor text  
❌ Overloading links in one section  
❌ Ignoring funnel structure  
❌ Leaving pages without inbound links  
❌ Linking to irrelevant pages  

---

# 10. Internal Linking Checklist

Before publishing, ensure:

- At least 2–5 internal links added
- Includes both contextual and funnel links
- Anchor text is descriptive and natural
- Links are relevant to the content
- No forced or awkward placement
- Supports SEO + user journey

---

# 11. Final Rule

Internal linking is not just for SEO.

It must:
- Guide users
- Build topic authority
- Support conversion

If links do not improve user experience → they are wrong.
