# Shopplaza Content Rules

## 1. Content Scope

Shopplaza blog content serves TWO parallel marketing systems:

### A. Website Marketing
- Drive organic traffic (SEO)
- Educate merchants
- Build topical authority
- Support funnel (TOFU → MOFU → BOFU)

### B. App Marketing (ASO Support)
- Support Shopify App Store rankings
- Drive installs from blog traffic
- Reinforce app use cases
- Bridge SEO → ASO → Conversion

👉 Every article must support at least ONE of these systems (ideally both)

---

## 2. Content Segmentation: Website vs App

### Website-driven Content
Focus:
- Educational
- Strategy-driven
- Problem-first

Examples:
- "How to increase AOV on Shopify"
- "What is Shopify B2B?"
- "How to optimize Shopify checkout"

Goal:
👉 Traffic + trust

---

### App-driven Content
Focus:
- Use cases
- Solutions
- Tools
- Comparisons

Examples:
- "Best Shopify apps for B2B"
- "Top apps to hide prices on Shopify"
- "How to add request a quote on Shopify"

Goal:
👉 Install intent

---

## 3. Writing Philosophy

All content must follow:

👉 Problem → Solution → Outcome

NOT:

❌ Feature → Feature → Feature

---

## 4. Tone of Voice

- Friendly, modern, and direct
- Growth-oriented
- Practical, not theoretical
- Focus on merchant outcomes

### Preferred language:
- "Increase conversions"
- "Scale your store"
- "Simplify your workflow"
- "Turn visitors into buyers"

---

## 5. Content Structure (Mandatory)

Every article must follow:

### 1. Hook
- Real problem or opportunity
- Must resonate immediately

### 2. Context
- Why it matters
- Impact on revenue / operations

### 3. Core Content
- Strategies OR solutions OR tools

### 4. Use Cases
- Real scenarios (B2B or B2C)

### 5. App Integration (if relevant)
- Natural placement
- Based on problem → solution

### 6. Internal Links
- Related blogs
- App pages

### 7. CTA
👉 "Try free on Shopify App Store"

---

## 6. Feature Positioning Rules

Never describe features without context.

❌ Weak:
- "This app has a quote feature"

✅ Strong:
- "Let customers request quotes directly from product pages so you can close high-value B2B deals faster"

---

## 7. App Mention Rules

Apps should be:
- Contextual
- Problem-driven
- Naturally introduced

Each mention must answer:
- What problem does this solve?
- Why is this better than alternatives?
- What outcome does it drive?

---

## 8. B2B vs B2C Content Rules

### B2C
Focus:
- Conversion
- UX
- AOV
- Marketing

Examples:
- Product quiz
- Instagram feed
- Digital downloads

---

### B2B
Focus:
- Workflow
- Pricing
- Access control
- Operations

Examples:
- Request a quote
- Hide price
- Company accounts

---

## 9. CTA Rules

Primary CTA:
👉 "Try free on Shopify App Store"

CTA placement:
- After solution sections
- At the end
- Optional mid-content CTA for long articles

---

## 10. Content Quality Rules

Every article must:
- Match search intent
- Provide actionable insights
- Include real examples
- Guide toward next step

---

## 11. What to Avoid

- Generic "10 tips" content
- Feature dumping
- No internal links
- No funnel awareness
- Over-promotional tone
