# Shopplaza SEO Rules

## 1. SEO System Overview

Shopplaza SEO operates on TWO layers:

### A. Website SEO
- Blog traffic
- Landing pages
- Topical authority

### B. App SEO (ASO Support)
- Drive installs via content
- Reinforce app keywords
- Support App Store ranking indirectly

---

## 2. Search Intent Framework

Every page must target ONE intent:

### Informational
- "What is Shopify B2B"
- "How to increase Shopify sales"

👉 Website SEO focus

---

### Commercial
- "Best Shopify apps for B2B"
- "Top Shopify quiz apps"

👉 Bridge SEO → ASO

---

### Transactional
- App pages
- Landing pages

👉 Direct conversion

---

## 3. Keyword Strategy

Each page must include:

- 1 primary keyword
- 3–5 secondary keywords
- Semantic variations

---

## 4. Page Types

### Blog Pages
- Target informational & commercial intent
- Drive traffic
- Support funnel

---

### Landing Pages
- Target commercial & transactional
- Drive conversion

---

### App-related Content
- Target app-related queries
- Support install decisions

---

## 5. On-Page SEO Rules

### Title
- Include primary keyword
- Benefit-driven
- Clear intent

---

### Headings
- Answer specific questions
- Structured logically

---

### Meta Description
- Encourage clicks
- Highlight outcome

---

## 6. Content Depth

Content must:
- Fully answer query
- Include examples
- Include use cases

---

## 7. Internal Linking (SEO)

Each page must:
- Link to related blogs
- Link to app pages

---

## 8. Entity Coverage

Content should naturally include:
- Shopify
- apps
- merchants
- conversions
- workflows
- B2B / B2C

---

## 9. SEO + ASO Connection

Blog content should:
- Reinforce app keywords
- Support app use cases
- Drive installs

---

## 10. Technical SEO Considerations

- Clear heading hierarchy
- Clean URL structure
- No duplicate content
- Fast loading pages

---

## 11. Avoid SEO Mistakes

- Keyword stuffing
- Ignoring intent
- No internal links
- Thin content
- Writing for bots
